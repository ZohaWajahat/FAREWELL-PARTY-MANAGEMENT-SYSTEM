# Farewell-Party-Management-System

# Introduction
The Farewell Party Management System is a comprehensive platform tailored for junior students to organize a memorable farewell event for their senior counterparts. This system not only
simplifies the event planning process but also facilitates the participation of teachers who can attend with their families. The objective is to create a wholesome experience, covering aspects such as attendee management, task assignments, budget tracking, and detailed planning of dinner, stage performances, decorations, and venue arrangements. This includes:<br>

## University-Wide Participation:
Enable all university students to suggest items for the dinner menu and volunteer for stage performances. Implement a voting system for menu items and performance proposals.<br>

The use cases are as follows:<br>

### Student Registration\Login:
• Students can suggest items for dinner menu.<br>
• Implement a strong voting mechanism for the suggested items.<br>
• The most popular item becomes the part of final menu.<br>

### Performance Volunteer
• Allow students to propose performances for the farewell Party.<br>
• Provide details such as performance type, duration, and any special requirements.<br>
• Voting can be introduced for selecting the most anticipated performances.<br>

### Teachers and family registration
• Teachers can register for the event and provide information about accompanying family members.<br>
• Teachers' registration is complimentary, and details about the number of accompanying family members should be captured.<br>

### Allocation of tasks:
Organizers can assign tasks related to decorations, venue setup, and other responsibilities to student volunteers. Progress tracking and completion status should be visible.<br>

### Attendance Tracking
• System administrators can track the attendance of students, teachers, and their families.<br>
• Generate reports on the number of attendees and any special requests or considerations.<br>

### Budget Tracking
• Implement a budget tracking system to monitor expenses related to venue booking, catering, decorations, etc.<br>
• Regularly update the budget status to avoid overspending.<br>

## Additional Description for Role-Based Use Cases for Farewell Party Management System
### Authentication
#### Actor:<br>
Senior Student<br>

#### Description:<br>
Authenticate into the system with a unique username and password.<br>

#### Use Case Steps:
• Enter username and password.<br>
• System verifies credentials.<br>
• Access granted to the relevant role-specific dashboard.<br>

### View/Manage/Edit Dinner Menu
#### Actors:<br>
Manager of Dinner Menu Team, Dinner Menu Team

#### Description:<br>
Create, modify, and manage the dinner menu for the farewell Party.

#### Use Case Steps:
#### • View Menu:

#### • Manage Menu:
